WHY DOES DRIVER FINDER WORK?

HERE'S WHY:

The answer to this question is an explanation of what we believe in and how that manifests itself in our process. We have learned a lot as we have recruited truck drivers for 15 years and most of those lessons are just common sense.

WE DONT FORGET A NAME

For fifteen years we have kept track of all the people we have talked to about driving a truck. Why? A driver may not be interested today in making a change or they might already have a job. No matter, we learn what we can learn today and remain patient. Which leads to another important lesson.

ONE MANS TRASH IS ANOTHER MANS TREASURE

A person who drives a truck is a treasure but their wants and needs may not match with our operational realities. No matter, that driver simply needs to find the right company to work for and suddenly the entire value of the driver’s potential can be unlocked.

TIMING IS EVERYTHING

You may offer the best opportunity in the world but if the timing is not right, it is as if no one sees it. We work seven days a week so you don’t have to, doing our best to communicate with drivers when it is convenient for them so they are receptive to the message we want them to hear.

IF YOU CANT MEASURE IT, YOU CANT MANAGE IT

In God we trust, all others bring data. Capturing the various measures at all stages of the process helps us understand what works and what does not work. We want to know not only what the problem might be but also what the causes are and how much effect they have. We can also measure user engagement to identify which members of the team are to be emulated and which users can improve.
